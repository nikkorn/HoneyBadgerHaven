Dietary Info

What They Eat

Honey badgers have been known to eat pasta containing balls of meat in a rich herby tomato sauce.

"That's a spicy meatball"
- Honey Badger, 2017

How Often They Eat

Young honey-badgers (AKA honey-badgettis) snack frequently. It causes their parents a lot of frustation because they fill up on snacks but don't have space for their dinner.

Adult honey-badgers eat only once in their adult life.

How Much They Eat

Honey-badgers eat 1/4 of their body weight at every meal. They need to eat so much because they are extremely inefficient. Even at resting they consume as much energy as 3 and a half boeing 747s.
