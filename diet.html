Dietary Info What They Eat Honey badgers have been known to eat pasta containing balls of meat in a rich herby tomato sauce. "That's a spicy meatball" - Honey Badger How Often They Eat Young honey-badgers (AKA honey-badgettis) frequently snack frequently. It causes their parents a lot of frustation because they fill up on snacks but don't have space for their dinner. Adult honey-badgers eat only once in their adult life. How Much They Eat Honey-badgers eat 1/4 of their body weight at every meal.
