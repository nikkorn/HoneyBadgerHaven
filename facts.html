Interesting Facts

Here are some interesting facts we've managed to dig up about the Honey Badger.

  • Every male Honey Badger is named Jimbob. No one can remember why.
  • Female Honey Badgers are either named Sheila or Shayla. Given the wide range of Honey Badger accents, it is often difficult to work out which one was said.
  • Honey Badgers prefer honey from bees that have visited daisies. One suggested reason why is that the taste of the daisy reminds them of the good old times.
  • Honey Badges hate laptops from HP, they would prefer it if the company were called HB.
